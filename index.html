About Me

Hello,
I am a Master's Student at the Computer Science and Engineering Department at Penn State University. Having done my undergrad in Electronics and Telecommunications , I have a strong hold over both computer and software engineering. My main objective for now is to find a full-time job where I can apply and learn all my software skills with an understanding of real world application.

Fresh out of my undergrad, i pursued my Master's the very same year, meaning little work experience. However to balance it out, I have been working on plenty of projects and also interning at FanDuel, Inc in the numberFire Data Analytics Team as a software engineer. I have learned most of the new technologies on my own (React.js, Angular.js, Java, Git etc.), applying them in some of my projects.Until recently ,I have been working on Web Development (Applications and Websites) MVC Frameworks(PHP) as a part of my internship program.

Apart from tech, I am also passionate about arts and sports. Being a guitarist, singer, writer and a pianist, I have been involved in bands and composition of my own songs. One of the main objectives in my learning run , is to be involved in something that fuses creativity and technology. I also play badminton(State Level Player from India, Penn State Badminton Champion'15) , Soccer, Roll Ball (National Level India) and squash.
